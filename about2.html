WHAT IS SHARE PAD?

SharePad aims to end the long-standing struggle with apartment hunting in the Philippines.

The main feature of SharePad is filtering its listing by the common interests of its users. This would extremely benefit the indiviuals looking to share a space with complete strangers.

Users can also downsize its searches via nearest proximity from their work location. This can cut their search time in half.
